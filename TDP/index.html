About

The WISPNET is an experimental LAN (Local Area Network) network created by William S. Popovici for the purpose of repairing, utilizing, and learning about technology.

Services

One of the many goals we have been working towards is Linux Mint-Basil, a modified distribution of Linux Mint which would be optimized for older or less powerful systems, such as Dell OptiPlex systems and Raspberry Pi systems.
