Home

This Test Development Site (TDS) will help further the learning of the WISPNET project as a practice workspace for various code implementations and improvements before they are released to the mainstream. The pre-release to this site is the Test Development Page (TDP) which may not always be optimized.

Updates may be posted at a later date.

About

The WISPNET is an experimental LAN (Local Area Network) network created by William S. Popovici for the purpose of repairing, utilizing, and learning about technology.

Services

One of the many goals we have been working towards is Linux Mint-Basil, a modified distribution of Linux Mint which would be optimized for older or less powerful systems, such as Dell OptiPlex systems and Raspberry Pi systems. The WISPNET's web design services are not yet fully operational, but if you are interested in any future related services, please contact us.

Projects

There are a few ongoing projects at WISPNET, such as the publicly available IOserver! IOserver is a collection of JavaScript-based singleplayer games created in the course of learning and entertainment.
